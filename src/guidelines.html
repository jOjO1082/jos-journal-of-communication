Call for Papers

Jos Journal of Media and Communication Studies is a peer-reviewed, refereed journal published twice a year (April and September) by the Department of Mass Communication, University of Jos, Nigeria. The journal welcomes conceptual, theoretical, philosophical and empirical articles in all areas of communication, particularly current issues relating to the communications industry.

Open Access & Publication Focus

Articles discussing broad issues anchored in communication or its related fields are accepted for publication consideration, as are book reviews. The journal is published online as an Open Access Model with two editions (April and September), and each edition contains between 15 to 20 articles.

Publication of the journal is regular and the theme for special editions shall be advertised if the need arises. Hard copies of the journal shall be made available on demand through negotiated terms.

Author Guidelines

Prepare your manuscript for review

Follow the journal's submission rules to ensure a smooth review process. Your paper should be original, clearly structured, and aligned with the scope of media and communication studies.

Manuscript Format

All manuscripts must be submitted in Microsoft WORD document format (including all tables, figures, and appendices).
NOTE: We do not accept PDF and other files format.

Typeset manuscript in A-4 paper size, 12 points for the font size, Times New Roman for the font type and 2.0 line spacing. The paper alignment should be ''Justified'' all through the content with 1.0 inch on the top, bottom, right, and left margins.

Manuscripts should be 5000-8000 words in length or 22 pages long – references are included in this estimate. Each article should also have an abstract of not more than 250 words and 3-5 keywords.

Manuscripts submitted must be original and should not be under consideration in any other journal or publication outlet.

Manuscripts should have on the first page the following information only:
  • Title of the paper
  • Name of Author(s) - First Name, Middle Name and Surname (Initials are not accepted)
  • Institutional affiliation, e-mail address and mobile telephone details.
  • Please, note that all these information must not appear on any other page in the entire article.

The second page should contain the title of the manuscript, the abstract and key words before the introduction.

Citations and references must conform to the seventh (7th) edition format of the APA style.

Authors are responsible for obtaining copyright waivers and permissions as may be needed for republication of any material, text, illustrations etc.

The manuscript must be written in the British English (Spelling).

Tables and figures should be inserted in the appropriate places in the body of the article.

Originality & Ethics

Your work must be original, unpublished, and compliant with our publication ethics expectations.
