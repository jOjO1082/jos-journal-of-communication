Article Processing Charges (APC)

Jos Journal of Media and Communication Studies (JJMCS) is an Open Access Publisher that provides free access to all the published materials to its users/readers. However, the publisher does not get any institutional or organisational support to meet its publication and archiving expenses. Hence, the publisher relies exclusively on article processing charges of the authors.

All manuscripts submission must be accompanied by an attachment of receipt of the peer review fee of Five Thousand Naira only paid into the journal's account below:

ACCOUNT NUMBER - 6060346657

ACCOUNT NAME - Jos Journal of Media and Communication studies

BANK - Fidelity Bank

Upon acceptance of the manuscript for publication, the authors will pay a publication fee of Thirty Thousand Naira only into the account and forward the receipt to the Editor via email before further action can be taken on the peer reviewed, corrected and resubmitted manuscript.
